The EUCAS lab is led by [Henry Brighton](https://www.tilburguniversity.edu/webwijs/show/h.j.brighton.htm) and located in the 
[Department of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence](http://csai.nl), [Tilburg University](http://www.uvt.nl), The Netherlands. 

<b> Our goal is to advance the understanding of cognitive and adaptive systems by examining how theories, models, and organisms cope with extended uncertainty conditions. </b> 

Our research cuts across disciplinary divisions to investigate fundamental questions in the study of rationality, decision science, 
linguistic theory, economic theory, and evolutionary and adaptive systems. We use of machine learning techniques, statistical modeling, and laboratory and online experiments with human participants to explore these issues. 

Please see the [Research](research) section for details about our approach and research contributions, and the [Publications](publications) section for information about specific publications.
